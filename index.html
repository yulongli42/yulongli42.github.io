I'm an incoming PhD student at MIT EECS advised by professors Leslie Kaelbling and Tomas Lozano-Perez, where I aspire to build intelligent robots. Previously, I obtained MS in Robotics from CMU and bachelor's degrees in CS, physics, and mathematics from Columbia with highest honors.

In my free time, my biggest hobby is making legendary plans about non-existant futuristic artifacts a.k.a. daydreaming. Besides mundane stuff like quantum computers, artificial memory, interstellar travelers, and time machines, my most pressing plan is to design a brain smart enough to earn a PhD. If I'm neither daydreaming or nightdreaming, I'm probably cycling or running.
