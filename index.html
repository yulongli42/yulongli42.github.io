In my free time, my biggest hobby is making legendary plans about non-existant futuristic artifacts a.k.a. daydreaming. Besides mundane stuff like quantum computers, artificial memory, interstellar travelers, and time machines, my biggest plan is to construct a much smarter brain in replacement of mine to earn a PhD degree. Though daydreaming is certainly time-consuming, I manage to squeeze out some time for cycling, saxophone, and poker.
