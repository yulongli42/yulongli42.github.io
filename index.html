I am an incoming master's student at the Robotics Institute of Carnegie Mellon University. I earned my bachelor's degrees in computer science, physics, and mathematics from Columbia University, where I graduated with the highest honors. During my time there, I had the privilege of working with Professor Shuran Song and Professor Henry Yuen.

In my free time, my biggest hobby is making legendary plans about non-existant futuristic artifacts a.k.a. daydreaming. Besides mundane stuff like quantum computers, artificial memory, interstellar travelers, and time machines, my most pressing plan is to design a brain smart enough to earn a PhD degree for me. Though daydreaming is undoubtedly time-consuming, I manage to squeeze out some time for cycling and poker.
